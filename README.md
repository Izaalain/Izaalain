# 💫 About Me:
I am currently helping non-profit community-based organizations in Central Pennsylvania to collect, analyze and visualize data in order to make informed decisions pertaining resource mobilization and  allocation.<br><br>One of the projects I am working on involves managing and analyzing data on bicycles for donation and recycling in Harrisburg. <br>The other project involves analyzing air quality data from low-cost sensors in relation to Environmental Protection Agency monitors to understand the air quality condition in Central Pennsylvania<br><br>Since 2025, I have been pursuing a Master of Computer Science in Data science at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign to build a strong foundation in Statistical Reasoning, Machine Learning, Data Mining, Data Visualization and Cloud Computing<br>I am happy to chat about best practices in data management, analysis and vizualization and how to help low-buget, non-profit organizations to make good use of data!
